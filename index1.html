MEDIA ARTS + PRACTICE SENIOR
Storytelling—primarily through the media of creative writing, graphic design, photography and film—is and always has been my passion. It is my goal, through a combined exploration of narrative-based devices and critical and filmic theories, to further my development as a storyteller and, along the way, create cross-media digital arguments that empower viewers and raise awareness about environmental and social issues around the world.
